Syntactically Awesome Style Sheets

What is SASS?

SASS stands for Syntactically Awesome Style Sheets. And they are just that, they're awesome!

What makes SASS stand out?

SASS has a ton of awesome features including:

  • Variables
  • Nesting
  • Mixins
  • Inheritence
And MORE! Check out all the details at sass-lang.com

How's it work?

Easy, that's how! SASS is a CSS pre-processor, meaning you write SASS, and it is processed into conventional CSS. Just tell Ruby to watch you SASS file, and as you make changes, Ruby will convert your SASS into CSS, saving you time and headaches!
